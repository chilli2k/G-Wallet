How to buy gold To buy gold on G-Wallet, follow these steps:

  1. Register into G-Wallet website providing the necessary credentials.
  2. Click on buy in the user dashboard.
  3. The current market buying price of gold (including tax) appears in the user dashboard. This price stays locked throughout the day.
  4. Enter the email id and the amount of gold you want to buy, in INR.
  5. Click on Buy and its done.
